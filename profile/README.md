# Tomato - Diag-Leaf

## What is Tomato - Diag-Leaf?
Tomato - Diag-Leaf is an AI-powered mobile application built with Flutter that helps users detect and classify diseases in tomato leaves using image processing and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN).

Designed with simplicity and accuracy in mind, Tomato - Diag-Leaf allows users to take or upload a picture of a tomato leaf, crop it as needed, and instantly get predictions of potential plant diseasesâ€”helping tomato farmers to take early action to protect their crops.
